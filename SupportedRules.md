# 2D Geometry Formulas

### Square
s=side
Area: A=s^2
Perimeter: P=4s

### Rectangle
l=length, w=width
Area= A=lw
Perimeter: P=2l+2w

### Triangle
b=base, h=height
Area: A=(1/2)bh
Perimeter: P=a+b+c

### Equilateral Triangle
s=side
Height: h=s(sqrt(3)/2)
Area: A=(sqrt(3)/2)s^2
Perimeter: P=3s

### Parallelogram
b=base, h=height, a=side
Area: A=bh
Perimeter: P=2a+2b

### Circle
r=radius, d=diameter
Diameter: d=2r
Area: A=PIr^2
Circumference: C=2PIr=PId

## Upper and Lower bounds rules.
UB=UB+UB
UB=UB*UB
LB=LB+LB
LB=LB*LB

UB=UB/LB
UB=UB-LB
LB=LB/UB
LB=LB-UB
